The Best Movies According to Rob

My top 5 movies of all-time

The Godfather

A classic, enough said.

The Dark Knight

My first favorite and will always be.

12 Angry Men

A simple yet moving film about persuasion and sticking to the facts. To add, I've seen a live performance of this film!

The Good, the Bad and the Ugly

The wild west, thrilling and a strong story. A slow burner that culminates in one of my favorite scenes ever.

Pulp Fiction

A story that you cannot predict! Each time I see this movie I am again struck at how much thought was put into the dialogue and pacing.

My top 3 movies I watched in 2023

The Menu

Thrilling and unpredictable. I didn't know what I was getting into when I started this film.

Asteroid City

Wes Anderson is a top director in my mind. His craft is art.

Oppenheimer

No words to describe how good this film is. Start to finish, thrilling!
